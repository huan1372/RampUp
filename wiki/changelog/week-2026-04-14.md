# Weekly Digest — 2026-04-14

## What Was Learned
Initial knowledge base seeded with core vLLM concepts and inference optimization techniques. Key findings from research:

- **vLLM v0.19.0** is the current release (April 2026). The V1 engine (default since v0.8.0) delivers 1.7x throughput over V0. Model Runner V2 is the new default execution path.
- **Prefix caching is essentially free** in V1 — <1% overhead at 0% hit rate. Always-on.
- **FP8 quantization** is nearly free on H100/H200/B200 hardware — 2x memory reduction with up to 1.6x throughput gain and minimal accuracy loss.
- **Competitive landscape**: vLLM leads on high-concurrency throughput (4,741 tok/s at 100 req), SGLang on stable ITL and multi-turn, TensorRT-LLM on single-request throughput.
- **Q2 2026 roadmap** focuses on: custom Helion kernels, torch.compile improvements, KV cache manager redesign, GB200 optimization, and vLLM-Omni disaggregated serving.
