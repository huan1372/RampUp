## Key Findings

1. **MRV2 is production-ready and default** as of v0.19.0. The blog post confirms the 56% throughput gain from GPU-native input preparation is real and measurable on GB200.
2. **P-EAGLE is a significant spec-decode improvement** — 1.69× over EAGLE-3 at low concurrency by making K=7 economical. Benefit shrinks at c=64, suggesting batching overhead offsets drafting savings.
3. **Async KV prefetch (arXiv 2504.06319) is novel and orthogonal** — not yet in vLLM but architecturally compatible. H20 results (1.97× E2E) significant for Chinese cloud deployments.
4. **Prefix caching is free in v0.19.0** — <1% overhead at 0% hit rate; safe to enable by default.
